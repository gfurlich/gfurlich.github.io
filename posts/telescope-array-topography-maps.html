Categories : Telescope Array, Research, Remote Sensing, Python, STRM, GDAL,

Both of the maps below were created using this Jupyter python notebook. Python GDAL module was used to import the raster. The Haversine formula was used to account for the curvature of the earth.

  • STRM Data
  • TA Topography Map
  • TA Shaded Relief Map

STRM Data

STRM stands for the Shuttle Radar Topography Mission. Data was accessed form Earth Resources Observation and Science (EROS) Center. The data was cropped to the Center Laser Facility (CLF) of the array. Data can also be found here which has a beautiful view of the grand canyon and the green and Colorado River through the Colorado Plateau in Utah.

TA Topography Map

Topographical map of TA using STRM data. TA is about 1400 meters above sea level surround by mountains as a part the great basin and range region of the USA. The FD with a arc 30 km arc of the field of view as blue triangles, SD sites as purple circles, and the CLF as an orange hexagon.

TA Shaded Relief Map

Shaded relief map of TA using STRM data. The geographical feature of Longridge, Black Rock Mesa, and the Drum Mountains which the FD sites are named for are apparent.
