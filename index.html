I am a remote sensing research scientist with the APEX research and development group at Lockheed Martin Space Systems. I specialize in image processing with machine learning, signal processing, algorithm development, data fusion, object detection, object tracking, and object state estimation. My technical contributions have lead to selection as a Lockheed Martin Recognized Technical Talent and to the generation of intellectual property. I have a PhD in Physics from the University of Utah (graduated 2020) where my thesis focused on the remote sensing of ultra-high energy cosmic ray interactions within the Earth's atmosphere.
