I am a remote sensing research with the APEX research and development group at Lockheed Martin Space Systems. I focus on signal processing, algorithm development, and machine learning methods with images. My technical contributions have lead to selection as a Lockheed Martin Recognized Technical Talent and intellectual property generation. I have a PhD in Physics from the University of Utah (graduated 2020) where I wrote my thesis on the remote sensing of ultra-high energy cosmic ray interactions within the Earth's atmosphere.
